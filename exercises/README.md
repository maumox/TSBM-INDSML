## Uncertainty

**Problem 1.**
A factory in Taiwan produces sensors. Data shows that 5% of the sensors are defective. If two sensors are randomly selected, what is the probability that both sensors are NOT defective?

**Problem 2.**
A startup developing medical equipment has a 70% probability of launching its product on time. If it launches on time, it has a 50% probability of becoming profitable within the next year. If it fails to do so, the probability of becoming profitable within the next year decreases to 20%.

- What is the probability that the startup will launch its product on time and become profitable within one year?
- What is the probability that the startup will be profitable within a year?
- If the startup is profitable, what is the probability that its product was launched on time?

**Problem 3.**
The probability density function of a random variable X is:

f(x)=2x; 0≤x≤1

- Verify that it is a valid function
- Calculate the expected value of x, E[x]
- Calculate E[x^2]
- Calculate var[x]
